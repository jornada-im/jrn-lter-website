Cross-sectional area of Mesquite as a function of biomass
=========================================================

From Ludwig et al. (1975), table 2, aboveground volume and spatial area
of an individual is given in terms of biomass, from regressions on field
measurements. The intent here is to get the cross-sectional area as a
function of biomass.
